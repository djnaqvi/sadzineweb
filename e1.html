In my lowest moments I wouldn't feel anything anymore.
I'd just feel numb.

And lonely
because everyone else could move on
and walk away when things got bad,
but I couldn’t escape from myself.

What helped me keep going
were those people who stuck with me
when I felt like giving up.

Who told me I wasn’t alone,
who told me it’s ok to not be ok.
